Customizable fast text search index with exact, prefix, and fuzzy word matching. Great for
filtering in-memory documents like autocomplete or typeahead. Results ranked by Levenshtein
distance by default; the distance function is pluggable (Damerau-Levenshtein built in).
Inverted and trie indexes; trie fuzzy walks the tree with row-min pruning. Unicode-safe
(code-point iteration) and accent-folding covers precomposed letters (ß→ss, ø→o, æ→ae, ...).
